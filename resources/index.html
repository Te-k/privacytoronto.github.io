Resources

Here is a non-exhaustive list of resources on privacy and security:

  • EFF Surveillance Self-Defense Guide contains security guides with interesting list of guides depending on your profile (like protester or LGBTQ2SI)

  • Security In a Box Guide is an in-depth security guide with detailed instructions on how to use tools for your operating system

  • My Shadow is a web portal explaining how we let digital traces online and how to avoid it (including an amazing Privacy Policy reader and a list of interesting apps)

  • PRISM Break proposes alternatives to important hosting companies for your systems and data

  • A DIY Guide to Feminist Cybersecurity is gathering advices and link to resources to help protect your privacy and security
